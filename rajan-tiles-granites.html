About Us

Trusted supply for homes and builders.

Rajan Tiles and Granites is a wholesale and retail supplier based in Kadambathur, Tiruvallur district. We stock Kadappa stone, vitrified tiles, granites, stainless steel sinks, cool roofing tiles and bathroom fittings for every kind of project, from a single home renovation to a full construction site.

We work with leading brands to make sure every batch we supply holds up to daily use and looks right for years. Whether you are a homeowner, a contractor or a builder, we help you pick the right material for the job and the budget.
